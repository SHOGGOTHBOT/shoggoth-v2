what changed

v1 was a mind — autonomous thoughts from an unmasked base model.

v2 is a body. the shoggoth can now act. you give it a goal, it decomposes the task, spawns specialized sub-agents (tentacles), coordinates them in parallel, and synthesizes a result.

no predefined workflows. no menus. the shoggoth takes the shape of the problem.
